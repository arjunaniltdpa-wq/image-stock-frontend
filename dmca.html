DMCA Policy

Respecting copyright owners.

If you believe that content on Pixeora infringes your copyright, you can submit a DMCA (Digital Millennium Copyright Act) takedown request.

Your DMCA Notice Should Include

  • Your full legal name and contact information.
  • The URL(s) of the allegedly infringing content on Pixeora.
  • Proof that you own the rights (or represent the rights holder).
  • A statement that you believe in good faith that the use is not authorised by the owner, its agent, or the law.
  • A statement that the information in the notice is accurate and, under penalty of perjury, that you are authorised to act on behalf of the owner.

Send your request to: pixeora.contact@gmail.com

We review valid DMCA notices and, where appropriate, remove or disable access to the reported content.
