What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly, remember preferences, and improve user experience.

How We Use Cookies

  • Improve website speed and performance

  • Deliver relevant advertisements

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Analytics Cookies: Used to understand traffic and usage

  • Preference Cookies: Store user settings

We may use third-party services such as Google Analytics and Google AdSense, which may store cookies on your device to provide analytics and advertising services.

Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain features of the website.
